The Mission: A New Field of Inquiry

This framework's primary purpose is to demonstrate that the physics of telic systems is a tractable, neglected, and existentially important domain that deserves serious research investment.

The V1.0 of Aliveness is not intended to be the final answer. It is a catalyst designed to bootstrap a new field of inquiry. The ultimate measure of its success will be the degree to which it becomes obsolete, superseded by the superior models and frameworks built by a thriving research community.

Therefore:

Whether this specific framework proves correct, serves as a "wrong but generative" catalyst, or is integrated into a broader synthesis matters less than ensuring the domain itself is no longer neglected.
