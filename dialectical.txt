## 3.3 CORE DEFINITIONS (From Chapter 8 - Canonical)

**CRITICAL: These are the ONLY valid definitions. Do NOT invent alternatives.**

### The Three Classes of Telic Systems (Chapter 8)

**Classification criterion:** Net effect on environmental organized complexity over specified timescale and system boundary.

**Class 1: PARASITE (Entropic Converter)**
- **Definition (Ch8):** Maintains internal order by consuming a higher-order telic system and converting it into lower-order state, resulting in NET DECREASE in total organized complexity
- **Simple version:** Destroys complexity
- **Example:** Virus (1 complex liver cell → 10,000 simple viral particles = net complexity decrease)
- **SORT signature:** [S- O+ R- T±] - pathological extremes
- **Fails:** Fecundity (growth destroys resource base) and Synergy (purely extractive)

**Class 2: AUTOTROPH (Homeostatic Converter)**
- **Definition (Ch8):** Maintains existence in state of dynamic equilibrium, where total organized complexity of environment remains ROUGHLY CONSTANT over time
- **Simple version:** Preserves/maintains complexity (homeostatic equilibrium)
- **Physics:** Replacement engine—converts resources into maintenance without systematically degrading OR upgrading ecosystem complexity
- **Examples:** Climax ecosystem (Amazon rainforest in equilibrium), Tokugawa Japan (250 years perfect stasis)
- **Can possess:** Integrity, Harmony, Synergy
- **FAILS:** Fecundity (pure T- Homeostasis, no T+ Metamorphosis)
- **Verdict (Ch8):** "Not fully Alive in generative sense. Masterpiece of preservation, but opted out of process of becoming."

**Class 3: SYNTROPE (Syntropic Converter)**
- **Definition (Ch8):** Maintains internal order by consuming free energy/lower-order systems and converting them into HIGHER, EMERGENT COMPLEXITY, resulting in NET INCREASE in environmental negentropic potential
- **Simple version:** Creates/expands NEW complexity
- **Physics:** Fountain of negentropy. Creates new niches. Unlocks higher levels of game.
- **Examples:** First photosynthetic cyanobacteria (created oxygen → enabled aerobic life), first land plants, Roman Republic infrastructure, Apollo Program
- **Possesses:** ALL four virtues (IFHS)
- **Verdict (Ch8):** "ONLY class that is fully Alive. State of being Syntrope IS physical manifestation of Aliveness."

**The Hierarchy (Ch8, exact quote):** "Parasites destroy complexity. Autotrophs preserve it. Syntropes expand it."
